Packed Pancakes

Description

simple protein packed pancake/waffel mix with no eggs and only water

Ingridents

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/4 cup protein powder
  • 1 tbsp baking poweder
  • 1 cup water

Directions

  1. Mix together all ingredients
  2. set grittle to 375 and place 2tbsp of batter on the grittle
  3. flip once bubbles stop forming and steam is visible
  4. top with any garnish and enjoy
